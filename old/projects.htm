FEATURED PROJECTS

Civil Engineering


 		 MDC South Hartford Conveyance & Storage Tunnel
Zuvic Carr assisted AECOM with design documents for the Metropolitan District (MDC) South Hartford Conveyance and Storage Tunnel (SHCST), which is part of MDC’s Clean Water Project. The SHCST will be an 18-foot diameter deep rock tunnel, 200 feet below the surface, that will link MDC member communities with the Hartford Water Pollution Control Facility. View video
 

 		 Goodwin College Riverside Campus
Zuvic Carr completed the site survey; site, civil, utility design; and associated town and state permitting for the construction of a 109,000 sq. ft. campus building located on the east bank of the Connecticut River. View video
 

 		 Connecticut River Academy Magnet High School
Zuvic Carr was selected by the project architect to provide civil engineering and permitting services for a new magnet high school on a 10.5 acre parcel in East Hartford.
 

 		 Metropolitan District Ultraviolet Disinfection Facility
Zuvic Carr's NICET IV Inspector provided full-time professional construction inspection assistance to Woodard & Curran during a 10-week, peak period of construction for the UV Disinfection Facility.
 

 		 Travelers Insurance Company Hangar Expansion
Zuvic Carr completed the site design associated with the 10,400 sq. ft. addition to the existing hangar and office area at Travelers’ Corporate Aviation Division.

 		 Fairway Crossing Active Adult Community
Zuvic Carr completed the site design and permitting for an 89-unit active adult community.
 

 		 Silas Deane Highway Hydraulic Capacity Improvements & Goff Brook Overflow Project
Zuvic Carr provided professional land surveying services to Malcolm Pirnie, Inc. for the Metropolitan District Silas Deane Highway Capacity Improvements Project.
 

 		 Incinerator Upgrades & Heat Recovery Project
Zuvic Carr was engaged by Black & Veatch to assist with the design of the Metropolitan District Hartford Water Pollution Control Facility incineration upgrades and heat recovery system.

 		 Slope Stabilization Along the Niantic River
Zuvic Carr provided design and CT DEP permitting of slope stabilization (800’) along the Niantic River.

 

Transportation Engineering


 		 Replacement of Bridge No. 068013
Zuvic Carr was selected by the Town of Woodbury to design a replacement for Middle Quarter Road Bridge over South Brook and associated roadway improvements. Contract drawings, specifications, quantity estimates, and cost estimates are being prepared in accordance with the Connecticut Department of Transportation State Local Bridge Program.

 		 Rehabilitation of Bridge No. 02114
Zuvic Carr prepared contract documents for the rehabilitation of the I-691 Bridge over Amtrak Yard and State Street in Meriden. The 426-foot bridge is a five-span structure built in 1970. It consists of prestressed concrete I-beams with a reinforced concrete deck supported by reinforced concrete abutments and piers.

 		 Rehabilitation of Bridge No. 00807
Zuvic Carr prepared contract documents for the rehabilitation of the Ridge Road Bridge in Wethersfield. The existing 96-foot bridge, built in 1942, is a two-span cast-in-place concrete rigid frame structure.

 		 Rehabilitation of Red/Yellow Loop Bridge and Replacement of Causeway Bridge
Zuvic Carr designed a major rehabilitation of two bridges at the Metropolitan District (MDC) Water Filtration site in West Hartford. The work was performed for GZA GeoEnvironmental as part of an ongoing MDC Contract (2011-23).

 		 Replacement of Bridge No. 05588
Zuvic Carr prepared contract documents for the replacement of the Route 74 Bridge over Hockanum River in Vernon. The original bridge was a dual cell corrugated metal pipe arch structure built in 1955 that carried two lanes of traffic over the River.

 		 Replacement of Bridge No. 02719
Zuvic Carr prepared contract documents for the replacement of the Route 147 Bridge over Lyman Meadow Brook in Middlefield. The 13-foot bridge was a single span structure built in 1920. It consisted of a cast-in-place concrete slab supported by stone masonry abutments.

 		 Riverside Drive Resurfacing
Zuvic Carr completed the design and construction administration for ±1,800 linear feet of public roadway rehabilitation, including the oversight of traffic counts and a detailed pavement condition investigation.
 


Environmental Engineering


 		 MDC South Hartford Conveyance & Storage Tunnel – QEP
Zuvic Carr is providing environmental consulting and engineering for the MDC South Hartford Conveyance and Storage Tunnel project, including Qualified Environmental Professional services, preparation of Soil, Tunnel Muck and Water Management Plans, preparation of a storm water pollution control plan and permit, and periodic sampling for compliance and soil disposal.
 
MDC South Hartford Conveyance & Storage Tunnel – PCBs
Zuvic Carr provided environmental engineering associated with PCBs for the MDC South Hartford Conveyance and Storage Tunnel project. Some of the soil fill at the tunnel launch shaft site contained PCBs and was subject to the requirements of TSCA. Up to 15 feet of soil over a 4 acre area required removal for construction. Zuvic Carr completed investigations, Notification of Cleanup, and verification sampling.
 

 		 Goodwin College Brownfields Redevelopment
Zuvic Carr completed site and environmental design and permitting for the construction of a new 109,000 sq. ft. main campus building located on the east bank of the Connecticut River. View video
 

 		 Pathways Academy for the Advancement of Design and Technology
Zuvic Carr provided professional environmental consulting and Licensed Environmental Professional (LEP) services in connection with the construction of a magnet school located on 4.5 acres of a former industrial property. The site was subject to the CT Transfer Act.

 		 Metropolitan District Wet Weather Expansion Project
Zuvic Carr assisted ARCADIS U.S. on the Metropolitan District Wet Weather Expansion Project. The purpose of this $350M project is to design new influent, primary, and wet weather treatment facilities at the Hartford Water Pollution Control Facility.

 		 Metropolitan District Warehouse Building Use Assessment
Zuvic Carr was retained by Hazen and Sawyer, P.C. to complete an asbestos and lead paint survey of a warehouse, as part of a building use assessment for the Metropolitan District.
 

 		 Limited Asbestos and Lead Paint Survey for the Rocky Hill Housing Authority
Zuvic Carr and its subcontractor completed an asbestos and lead paint survey of housing for elderly citizens maintained by the Rocky Hill Housing Authority.

 		 Laidlaw Bus Terminal
Zuvic Carr helped the owner convert the former Tobacco Valley Sanitation (TVS) facility into a modern school bus facility.
