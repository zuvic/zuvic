FEDERAL, STATE AND LOCAL PERMITTING

Local Permit Submissions and Presentations

Zuvic Carr prepares applications and presentations to local boards, commissions, and land use agencies including municipal:

  • Planning and Zoning Commissions
  • Inland Wetlands and Watercourses Commissions
  • Conservation Commissions
  • Historic District Commissions
  • Zoning Board of Appeals
Our firm understands local regulatory agencies. Some of our key staff have served as municipal land use agency commissioners in the past.

State Permit Applications

We prepare the following State of Connecticut Department of Energy and Environmental Protection (CTDEEP) Individual Permit Applications and submissions:
 
  • Discharge Permits to Groundwater, Sanitary Sewers (POTW), Surface Waters (NPDES)
  • Dam Safety, Flood Management Certifications, Inland Wetlands and Watercourses, Inland 401 Water Quality Certification, Water Diversion
  • Office of Long Island Sound Certificate of Permission, Coastal 401 Water Quality Certification, Structures and Dredging/Fill/Tidal Wetlands
  • Solid Waste Facilities, Hazardous Waste Management/Treatment/Storage and Disposal Facilities
Zuvic Carr also prepares the following CTDEEP General Permit Registrations/Authorizations:
 
  • Discharge of Groundwater Remediation Wastewater to a Sanitary Sewer or a Surface Water, Domestic Sewage, Food Processing Wastewater, Stormwater Associated with Commercial Activities, Stormwater & Dewatering Wastewaters from Construction Activities, Stormwater from Small Municipal Separate Storm Sewer Systems (MS4), Vehicle Maintenance Wastewater
  • Emergency/Temporary Authorization for Discharge to POTW, Surface Water, Groundwater
  • Programmatic General Permits for Inland Water Resources, Office of Long Island Sound Programs
  • Contaminated Soil and/or Staging Management (Staging/Transfer)
  • Special Waste Authorization to Landfill, Resource Recovery Facility (RRF) Disposal
  • In-Situ Groundwater Remediation:  Enhance Aerobic Biodegradation, Chemical Oxidation
  • Emergency Transport Authorization
We also help our clients obtain state transportation agency approvals:
 
  • State Traffic Commission (STC) Certificates

Federal Permit Applications

Zuvic Carr prepares applications for permit approvals from the:
 
  • U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (US ACOE)
  • Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)
