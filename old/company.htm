Zuvic, Carr and Associates, Inc. (Zuvic Carr) is a full service, multidisciplinary engineering and consulting firm located in central Connecticut. For 30 years, Zuvic Carr has provided civil and environmental engineering services throughout Connecticut and in other states. Because of our size and company structure, we are able to provide customer focused and cost-effective consulting services.

Our staff includes licensed professional engineers, Connecticut licensed environmental professionals, licensed land surveyors, certified asbestos inspectors, environmental scientists, and leadership in energy and environmental design (LEED) professionals. Our professional staff and comprehensive slate of services allows us to respond to our clients’ needs quickly while keeping the project on schedule and on budget.

Our firm is prequalified by the Connecticut Department of Transportation (CT DOT) for: Bridge & Structure Design, Highway Design, and Construction Engineering & Inspection services for Road and Bridge. We are a Connecticut Department of Administrative Services (CT DAS) certified Small, Woman, and Minority Business Enterprise (SBE/WBE/MBE), and New York State certified WBE and MBE.

Our clients include:

  • Institutions of Higher Education
  • Corporations
  • Small Business Owners
  • Insurance Companies
  • State and Federal Agencies
  • Municipalities
  • Attorneys
  • Developers
  • Banks and Lending Institutions

Zuvic Carr understands that combining technical skill and innovative thinking with thorough project research and planning is essential for project success. We are devoted to creating the best solutions through our technical resourcefulness, our pursuit of excellence, and our responsiveness to each client's needs.
