PROVIDING CLIENT SATISFACTION SINCE 1988
For 30 years, Zuvic, Carr and Associates, Inc. (Zuvic Carr) has provided civil and environmental services throughout Connecticut. With an inspired and knowledgeable staff of professionals, we offer state-of-the-art expertise in a broad spectrum of engineering disciplines. Zuvic Carr is dedicated to using this expertise to tailor effective solutions that are not only practical but are consistent with our clients' needs.

SITE/CIVIL ENGINEERING SERVICES

Since 1988,  Zuvic Carr has been providing quality civil engineering services, ranging from the initial land surveying and site feasibility phase through site design and utility upgrades. We offer rapid engineering assistance as well as straightforward solutions to any complex land development project.

ENVIRONMENTAL ENGINEERING SERVICES

Zuvic Carr offers a variety of environmental engineering services for the private sector, municipalities, military, state government, and other clients. From initial environmental reviews and building surveys, to complex soil and groundwater remediation projects, we have the knowledge and experience to assist you.

LOCAL, STATE AND FEDERAL PERMITTING

Zuvic Carr has provided hundreds of local, state, and federal permit applications for a wide variety of public and private sector projects. We provide other regulatory agency support services including presentations at local and state public meetings/hearings and consent order assistance. View our full range of permitting services.

WATER & WASTEWATER ENGINEERING SERVICES

Zuvic Carr provides quality water and wastewater engineering services, including services at Wastewater Pollution Control Facilities and Water Treatment Plants.

TRANSPORTATION AND TRAFFIC ENGINEERING SERVICES

Zuvic Carr provides a variety of transportation and traffic engineering services, including bridge and highway design for local and state infrastructure. Our staff is familiar with the Federal Local Bridge Program and other federally and state funded programs. We are prequalified by the Connecticut Department of Transportation (CT DOT) to provide Bridge & Structure Design and Highway Design services.

CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

Zuvic Carr provides construction administration and part-time or full-time inspection services for private and public projects, including roads, bridges, and buildings. We are prequalified by CT DOT to provide Construction Engineering & Inspection services for Road and Bridge.

Contact us to learn more about our services, staff, and our ability to develop solutions.
