ENVIRONMENTAL PROJECT MANAGER/PE

Zuvic Carr is seeking a highly qualified Professional Engineer (PE) to join our growing environmental investigation and remediation team. The ideal candidate for the position is an energetic and experienced environmental professional with a strong seller-doer aptitude and desire to create strong client relationships. Responsibilities include site investigation and remediation, project management, remediation design supervision, proposal and scope development, client management, and report preparation.

Qualifications:
  • Professional Environmental Engineering License (PE) required
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Environmental Engineering (Master’s Degree a plus)
  • Licensed Environmental Professional (LEP) a plus
  • Experience and knowledge of applicable laws in Connecticut and New York, including TSCA
  • Self-starter, able to complete tasks with minimal supervision and direction
  • Strong professional judgment and commitment to producing a high quality product
  • Proven track record in creating and maintaining key client relationships
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills
