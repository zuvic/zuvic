WHO WE ARE
Zuvic Carr’s multidisciplinary approach is what makes us your primary trusted partner for all your project needs. Our ability to coordinate between disciplines and experts for any project assures you have the most effective solutions to your needs — solutions that anticipate the potential risks and mitigating factors as the project progresses from one phase to the next.

Our professional staff and comprehensive slate of services allows us to respond to our clients’ needs quickly while keeping the project on schedule and on budget. Because of our size and company structure, we are able to provide customer focused and cost-effective consulting services. This will decrease the amount of time needed to spend managing various stakeholders, and help you focus your efforts on what matters most. No matter the project you engage us for, Zuvic Carr can promise three consistent outcomes: complete solutions, superior responsiveness, and projects done right, on time, and on budget.

Marici Zuvic
President Ms. Zuvic, the founder of Zuvic, Carr and Associates, Inc. (Zuvic Carr) has over 30 years of experience as a municipal and highway engineer. She earned a B.S. in Civil Engineering from the University of Hartford and a B.S. in Civil Engineering Technology from the Technical State University in Santiago, Chile. Ms. Zuvic worked for the Connecticut Department of Transportation for over 20 years. Her experience includes major highway and bridge construction inspection, design of a variety of transportation related projects, bridge safety review, and consultant/municipal coordination.
Robert J. Carr, P.E., LEP
Chief Operating Officer Mr. Carr serves as principal-in-charge of environmental projects at Zuvic Carr. He has over 25 years of experience as an environmental engineer and consultant working for private sector clients as well as local and state agencies in Connecticut, New York and Rhode Island. Mr. Carr’s expertise includes all phases of environmental site assessments, The Connecticut Transfer Act, environmental compliance, and the design and permitting of soil and groundwater remediation systems. Mr. Carr also has conducted numerous environmental compliance audits for various industrial and commercial facilities in Connecticut and has authored several professional articles. He is a licensed professional engineer in Connecticut, New York, and Rhode Island and a licensed environmental professional (LEP) in Connecticut.
Tomek Grajewski, P.E.
Vice President Mr. Grajewski is responsible for special multidisciplinary projects and has over 30 years of experience as a civil engineer and project manager. He earned a B.S. degree in civil engineering from the Gdansk Polytechnic Institute and has completed advanced studies at the University of Connecticut. He is a licensed professional engineer and a licensed asbestos consultant/inspector. Mr. Grajewski has managed multidisciplinary projects worth more than $60 million. He has extensive experience working with numerous Connecticut State Agencies and large corporations. He has a broad range of experience in site planning, design, and permitting.

Victoria L. Man, LEP

Miroslav Grajewski, P.E.

Mr. Grajewski is responsible for business development and client relations. His professional engineering experience includes project management, design, and oversight of master plan development on a variety of projects throughout Connecticut and New England. Mr. Grajewski earned a B.S. in Civil Engineering from the University of Connecticut and has served as a Civil Engineer since joining the firm in 2012.
